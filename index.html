Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Rapiddon marketplace.

Instead of the platform giving you a fixed price or auto-assigning a worker, you post the job description. Verified nearby workers see the request and submit individual offers with their hourly rates and estimated arrival times. You review their prices, ratings, and distances side-by-side, then choose the one that works best for you.

Yes, safety and trust are our top priorities. All workers joining Rapiddon must undergo a registration check, including phone number verification, Aadhaar verification (Identity Check), and PAN card verification (Tax & Finance verification). We also track user ratings and reviews closely.

To avoid price gouging (overcharging during emergencies or heavy rain) and unfair wage competition (undercutting rates beneath living wages), Rapiddon implements an automated allowed range. For example, if the average plumber rate is ₹350/hr, our smart range allows bids between ₹250/hr and ₹450/hr.

Rapiddon relies on a commission-based model. We deduct a 10% to 15% service fee from the worker's earnings upon successful job completion. We also offer a premium subscription tier (₹299/month) for service partners to receive top visibility, a badge, and priority bid alerts.
